Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our pricing and services.

What's included in the one-time payment?

The one-time payment includes complete website development, design, testing, deployment, and the specified support period. No hidden fees or recurring charges for the development work.

Do you offer monthly payment plans?

Yes, we offer flexible payment plans. You can split the total cost into 2-3 installments based on project milestones. Contact us to discuss payment options that work for your budget.

What happens after the support period ends?

After the included support period, you can choose to extend support with our maintenance packages starting at $99/month, or handle updates yourself. We'll provide all necessary documentation and training.

Can I upgrade my plan later?

Absolutely! You can upgrade your plan at any time. We'll calculate the difference in pricing and apply it to additional features or pages. All existing work is preserved during upgrades.

How long does development take?

Development time varies by package: Starter (2-3 weeks), Professional (4-6 weeks), Enterprise (8-12 weeks). Timeline depends on complexity, content readiness, and revision rounds.
